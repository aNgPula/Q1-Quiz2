Inside Out 2

Inside Out 2 is about a teenager, Riley, going through puberty while she goes to a hockey camp with her friends. Compared to Riley in Inside Out, she has some new emotions! These emotions though take over her especially during trainings... This causes her to grow as a person, to grow into herself!

Reasons why I love this movie:

  • I like how relatable this movie is, especially with Anxiety. As a teenager, I do relate a lot to this movie more than I thought I would.

  • I love the way they portrayed Riley doing so much so that she is seen as "cool", so much so especially during her breakdown. (It was so emotional to the point my relative cried)

  • I am amazed on how they showed how surprising it is when puberty hits, because it really is. When we are young, we tend not to care about anything, but when we grow up, it hits us really.
